The Earth!!!

Winter in Kashmir is a mesmerizing spectacle that transforms the picturesque landscape into a magical wonderland. Nestled in the northern part of India, Kashmir experiences a winter characterized by heavy snowfall, freezing temperatures, and an ethereal beauty that captivates the senses. As the season unfolds, the majestic Himalayan mountains and the serene Dal Lake become blanketed in a pristine layer of snow, creating a postcard- perfect scene. The iconic houseboats, usually vibrant with colors, are adorned with a white, powdery coat, adding to the enchantment.
